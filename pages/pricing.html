Good to Know

Pricing Questions

Can I pause or cancel my subscription?
Absolutely — manage everything from your account. Pause for a holiday, skip a delivery, or cancel outright with no fees or fuss.
How far in advance should I book catering?
Two weeks is ideal for the coffee cart and private hire; pastry platters we can often turn around in 48 hours. Busy weekends book up fast, so earlier is safer.
Do you cater dietary requirements?
Yes. We always offer vegan and vegetarian options, and can accommodate gluten-free and common allergies with a little notice — just tell us when you enquire.
Is delivery really free?
Local delivery (within Maple Grove) is free on all subscriptions. Nationwide is a flat rate, and it's included free on the Household plan and up.

Not Sure Which to Pick?

Tell us how much coffee you get through and what you've got planned — we'll point you to the right option.
