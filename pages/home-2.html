The Craft

How the Magic Happens

Four steps from raw green coffee to the cup in your hand.

1

Source

We buy direct from farms and co-ops, paying above market for quality and care.

2

Roast

Small 12kg batches on our drum roaster, profiled to bring out each origin's best.

3

Rest

Beans rest 3–5 days to degas, so your first cup is balanced, not raw.

4

Brew

Pulled at the bar or bagged for home within days of roasting. Always fresh.
