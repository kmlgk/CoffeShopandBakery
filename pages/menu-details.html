Our Signature Espresso

The Kettle Street Blend — the cup that started it all, and still the heart of everything we pour.

House Signature since 2011

Balanced, Comforting, Endlessly Drinkable

The Kettle Street Blend is built for milk but confident enough to drink straight. We blend a washed Colombia for sweetness and clarity with a natural Brazilian for body and that unmistakable chocolatey finish. It's the flavour our regulars taste in their sleep.

Roasted in small batches on our drum roaster and rested for five days before it ever hits the hopper, it pulls a syrupy shot with a deep hazelnut crema — forgiving enough for a home machine, refined enough for the pros.

Tasting Notes

Cocoa Toasted Hazelnut Brown Sugar Baked Apple Smooth Finish

Questions We Hear a Lot

Is this blend good for filter too?
It's tuned for espresso and milk, but it makes a lovely, comforting cafetière or moka pot. For pour-over, we'd point you to our single-origin shelf.
How fresh are the retail bags?
Every bag is stamped with its roast date and rested, not stale — we sell within two weeks of roasting and recommend using within a month of opening.
Do you grind to order?
We do — tell us your brew method at the counter (espresso, filter, French press, moka) and we'll dial the grind in for you.
